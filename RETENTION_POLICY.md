# Data Retention Policy

## 1. Goal
To ensure the long-term availability and traceability of research data in accordance with open science principles.

## 2. Retention Periods
- **Raw Data**: Retained indefinitely in the GitHub repository and archived in Zenodo (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19145316).
- **Cleaned and Analysis-Ready Data**: Retained indefinitely with every major release.
- **Log Files and Temporary Artifacts**: Retained for a minimum of 2 years from the date of release.

## 3. Storage Infrastructure
GitHub (for active development) and Zenodo (for long-term archival).
