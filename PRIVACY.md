This repository does not collect or store personal information (PI) or personally identifiable information (PII).

All datasets are synthetic and do not correspond to any individual person. No privacy risk is associated with the use of this data.

The authors use external platforms (GitHub, Zenodo, Figshare) which may have their own privacy policies. Users are encouraged to review those policies separately.
