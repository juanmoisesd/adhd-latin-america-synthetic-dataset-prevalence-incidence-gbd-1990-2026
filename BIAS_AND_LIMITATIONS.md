# Bias and Limitations

## 1. Synthetic Nature
The primary limitation is that the data is **synthetic**. While it mimics real-world trends, it should not be used for direct clinical decisions without further validation against local epidemiological surveys.

## 2. Model Bias
The simulation is heavily influenced by Global Burden of Disease (GBD) assumptions. If the underlying IHME models contain biases (e.g., under-reporting in certain rural regions), these biases will be reflected in the synthetic dataset.

## 3. Geographical Granularity
Data is only available at the national level. Intranational variations (e.g., state-level differences in Brazil) are not captured in this version.

## 4. Temporal Projections
Values for 2024-2026 are projections and carry higher uncertainty than the historical 1990-2023 estimates.
