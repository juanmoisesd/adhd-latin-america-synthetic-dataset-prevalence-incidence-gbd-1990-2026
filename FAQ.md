# FAQ

## 1. Is this real data?
No, it is synthetic data generated to mimic IHME/GBD trends.

## 2. Can I use this for my thesis?
Yes, under the CC0 license you are free to use it for any purpose. We request appropriate citation.

## 3. How often is the data updated?
Updates are released periodically as new regional trends are published by the GBD consortium.
