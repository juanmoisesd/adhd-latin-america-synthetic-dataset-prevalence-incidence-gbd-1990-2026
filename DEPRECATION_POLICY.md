# Deprecation Policy

## 1. Context
To provide a stable environment for researchers, the ADHD dataset and its associated framework follow a formal deprecation process.

## 2. Versioning Policy
We use [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/) (vMAJOR.MINOR.PATCH).
- **MAJOR**: Breaking changes to the dataset structure or schema.
- **MINOR**: New data years or countries, new features without breaking backward compatibility.
- **PATCH**: Data corrections or minor documentation updates.

## 3. Deprecation Timeline
A major version will be maintained and supported for at least **12 months** after a new major version is released. Deprecated versions will remain archived on Zenodo but will not receive updates.

## 4. Communication
Deprecation notices will be posted in the `CHANGELOG.md` and through the repository's main README.
