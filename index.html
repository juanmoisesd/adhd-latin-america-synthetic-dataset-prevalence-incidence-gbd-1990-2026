📋 Overview

This synthetic dataset provides estimates of ADHD (Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder) prevalence, incidence, and total case numbers across Latin American countries for the period 1990–2026, based on Global Burden of Disease (GBD) methodology.

The dataset was generated as part of an open science initiative to support researchers, public health professionals, and policymakers working on neurodevelopmental disorders in the Latin American region. All values are synthetic estimates derived from GBD modelling approaches and are released into the public domain for unrestricted reuse.

This resource is part of the Research Hub maintained by Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya at juanmoisesdelaserna.es.

📊 Key Variables

The dataset captures three core epidemiological metrics per country and year:

VariableTypeDescription
countryStringLatin American country name
yearIntegerReference year (1990–2026)
prevalence_rateFloatADHD prevalence (%) in the population
incidence_rateFloatNew ADHD cases per 100,000 per year
total_casesIntegerEstimated total number of ADHD cases
age_groupStringAge stratification (where applicable)
sexStringSex disaggregation (where applicable)
