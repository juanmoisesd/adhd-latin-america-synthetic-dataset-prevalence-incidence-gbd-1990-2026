# Units and Conventions

## 1. Units of Measurement
- **Prevalence**: Expressed as a percentage of the total population (e.g., 5.25%).
- **Incidence**: Expressed as new cases per 1,000 person-years or absolute new cases per year depending on analysis context.
- **n_cases**: Absolute integer count of estimated cases.
- **Time**: Calendar years (A.D.).

## 2. ISO Standards
- **Countries**: Named using common English names. ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 codes are available in the mapping section of the documentation.
- **Dates**: YYYY format.

## 3. Precision
- Float values are rounded to 3-5 decimal places for prevalence and incidence.
- Integers for absolute counts.
