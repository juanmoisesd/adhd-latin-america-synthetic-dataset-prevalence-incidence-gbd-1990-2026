# ADHD in Latin America: Synthetic Dataset (1990-2026)

This repository provides a high-standard research dataset on ADHD prevalence, incidence, and case numbers in Latin America. It follows the "0.001% standard" for research data management, ensuring maximum FAIRness, provenance, and security.

## Repository Structure
The repository is organized into several functional groups:
- **A. Identity & Citation**: READMEs, Abstracts, and Citation metadata.
- **B. Legal & Ethics**: License, Terms of Use, and Privacy policies.
- **C. FAIR Metadata**: Interoperable metadata (DataCite, Schema.org, RO-Crate).
- **D. Data & Semantics**: Raw and cleaned data, codebooks, and schemas.
- **E. Provenance**: Lineage and transformation history.
- **F. Quality**: Validation reports and integrity tests.
- **G. Reproducibility**: Environment definitions and examples.
- **H. Security**: Cryptographic signatures and supply chain attestations.
- **I. Governance**: Operations and risk register.
- **J. Automation**: CI/CD workflows.
