# Data Protection Statement

## 1. Governance
The project follows international data protection standards, including GDPR and the Data Protection Laws of Spain (LOPD), as applied to research datasets.

## 2. Integrity and Security
Data integrity is maintained through cryptographic manifest files and signatures. See Group H for details.

## 3. Data Sensitivity
The ADHD data in this repository is **non-sensitive**, being synthetic and aggregated at the national level. Refer to `SENSITIVE_FIELDS.md` for more information on the fields used.
