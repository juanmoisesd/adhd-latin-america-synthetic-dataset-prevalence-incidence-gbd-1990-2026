# Ethics Statement

## 1. Ethical Standards
This research and the resulting synthetic dataset adhere to the highest ethical standards for public health and epidemiology research.

## 2. No Human Participants
The data in this repository is **entirely synthetic**, generated via simulation based on regional GBD trends. No individual human data was used directly to create this dataset.

## 3. Benefits to Society
The goal is to provide a baseline for analyzing ADHD trends in Latin America, helping policymakers and healthcare professionals understand the estimated burden of the disorder.

## 4. Addressing Bias
We acknowledge potential biases in the underlying GBD models and regional reporting differences. Refer to `BIAS_AND_LIMITATIONS.md` for details.
