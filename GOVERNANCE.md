# Governance Model

## 1. Decision Making
Decisions regarding dataset updates and framework changes are made by the Principal Investigator (Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya) in consultation with technical contributors.

## 2. Stewardship
Data stewardship follows FAIR principles. Metadata is maintained to ensure long-term discoverability.

## 3. Versioning Control
Major changes require a consensus review and must be documented in `RELEASE_NOTES.md`.
